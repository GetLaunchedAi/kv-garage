Refunds — KV Garage

We want you to be confident buying from KV Garage. Refunds are handled as follows:

Before Fulfillment (Pre-shipment)

Full refunds available on request within 48 hours of payment.

After We Purchase / Before Shipping

If an item was a preorder tied to a supplier/pallet and we have already paid the supplier, we will offer either (a) a full refund minus any non-refundable supplier/transaction fees, or (b) a swap to a different pack of equal or greater value. We will notify you immediately if this applies.

After Shipping / Delivery

Refunds for delivered items are only issued in case of major misrepresentation (items materially different than described) and must be reported within 7 days of delivery with photos. In those cases we will either (a) accept return and refund, or (b) offer an equal-or-better replacement. Return shipping for buyer-initiated returns is the buyer's responsibility unless we agree otherwise.

To request a refund: Email support@kvgarage.com with your order number and photos (if applicable). We will respond within 24 hours.

Swap / Inventory Assurance (2X Swap Program)

2X Swap Program — KV Garage

We stand behind the resale value of our packs. If you do not generate at least 2X your pack spend (e.g., $500 → $1,000) within 60 days from the delivery date, we will provide a one-time inventory swap of equal or greater retail value, subject to these rules:

  • You must provide evidence of attempted resale (2 sample listings OR 30 days of documented effort across marketplaces). We require reasonable proof to qualify for a swap.
  • Swaps are valid once per pack purchase and must be requested within 60 days of delivery.
  • Swapped inventory will be of equal or greater estimated resale value; we may substitute SKUs to ensure sellability.
  • This program is not intended to cover buyer inaction or marketplaces that are temporarily down — it protects against genuinely unsellable or misrepresented inventory.

To request a swap: Email swaps@kvgarage.com with your order number, links/screenshots showing resale attempts, and a short description of your actions. We typically resolve swap requests within 72 hours.

Guarantee & Seller Promises

Our Promise to You

  • No blind pallets. We curate and assemble packs from known SKUs — buyers get predictable mixes (cases/cables/protectors) unless a pack is explicitly labeled "auction contingent."
  • Clear ROI math. Every pack listing includes sample resale estimates so you can evaluate profit potential.
  • Quality checks. We inspect sample units and conduct basic QC. For larger orders we can arrange third-party inspections (additional fee).
  • Fast communication. We reply to support requests within 24 hours on business days.
  • Local pickup option. When available we offer local pickup so you don't pay shipping.

Transparency Policy

Transparency – What We Disclose

Listings explicitly show: Pack contents (by count and category), Price, Estimated resale value, Availability (In Stock / Pre-Order), and Expected ship/pickup window.

Pre-Order (Auction Contingent) listings clearly state:

"This pack is a pre-order for an upcoming lot. A deposit of X% reserves priority allocation. If we fail to secure the lot or content that differs materially, you will be offered a full refund, an equal-value swap, or store credit."

  • We never hide supplier manifests; upon request we will share proof of sourcing or photos of received stock.
  • Reviews & testimonials are real customer feedback; we may display anonymized reseller results with permission.

Compliance

  • CAN-SPAM / email: always include a way to unsubscribe in email and the business contact information. Use only lists you have permission to contact if sending mass emails.
  • SMS: include opt-out text: "Reply STOP to opt out." Keep SMS short and transactional. Only text numbers you have a business reason to contact.
  • Platform rules: don't mass-spam DMs on Instagram/Facebook — personalize messages to avoid bans.
  • Data privacy: store personal data only in your Google Sheet CRM and don't share contact lists without owner approval.

Shipping & Handling

  • All reseller packs ship within 3–5 business days of payment.
  • Tracking number provided within 24 hours of shipping.
  • Damaged shipments must be reported within 48 hours with photos for replacement.

Dispute Resolution

  • Our priority is long-term reseller success.
  • Any disputes are first handled through replacement/credit toward future packs.
  • By purchasing, you agree refunds are issued as credit, not cash back.
