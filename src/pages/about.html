About Kv Garage

Kv Garage is a community-driven liquidation business built on transparency, value, and helping resellers succeed. We source hand-picked liquidation pallets, mobile accessories, and tech deals to help entrepreneurs turn inventory into income.

What sets us apart? We combine local advantage with nationwide reach, offering verified inventory and exclusive discounts for resellers. Our founder's journey from Boy Scout popcorn sales to million-dollar monthly revenue drives our commitment to community growth and success.

I'm a 21-year-old entrepreneur who got hooked on reselling in 3rd grade as the top-selling Boy Scout popcorn seller. At 18, I joined a home improvement marketer and helped drive over a million dollars in monthly sales. Today, I run three businesses: liquidation supply chain, index investing, and consulting small business owners.
